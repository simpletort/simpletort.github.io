What is SimpleTort

One Platform. Legal, Healthcare & Insurance.

SimpleTort is an intelligent case and workflow management platform built on Google Cloud Platform and Monday.com — engineered for organisations that manage high volumes of complex cases, documents, and deadlines across legal, healthcare, and insurance operations.

We replace fragmented spreadsheets, siloed tools, and manual handoffs with a single intelligent system. Whether you are running mass tort campaigns, workers' comp claims, personal injury cases, healthcare compliance workflows, or insurance claims operations — every task, deadline, document, and communication is tracked, automated, and surfaced to the right person at the right time.

AI-extracted medical summaries from clinical and specialty records in minutes
Automated deadline tracking for filing windows, enrollment periods, and court dates
Role-based workflows for admin staff, paralegals, junior and senior partners
Integrated settlement calculator synced to your accounting system in real time

How It Works

From first contact to final settlement

SimpleTort handles your entire case pipeline in six automated stages — with AI assistance and structured human review checkpoints at every step.

01

Lead Captured

Intake from marketing partner. Auto-screened against campaign eligibility criteria.

02

Client Onboarded

Portal link issued. Client completes intake questionnaire and uploads required documents.

03

AI Processes Records

Medical and supporting documents analyzed by AI. Qualification score generated in minutes.

04

Paralegal Reviews

Auto-assigned paralegal reviews AI summary, verifies document completeness, advances case.

05

Attorney Approves

Junior partner reviews and signs off. Complex or high-value cases escalate to senior partner.

06

Settled & Disbursed

Settlement calculated, accounting synced, disbursement tracked to client and firm.

Security & Compliance

Enterprise-grade security, built from day one

SimpleTort handles sensitive Protected Health Information (PHI) and confidential legal records for thousands of clients. Our architecture was designed with HIPAA compliance, PHI encryption, and full audit logging as core requirements — not afterthoughts.

All PHI is encrypted at rest and in transit. Staff authenticate via your identity provider with mandatory multi-factor authentication. Every access event, status change, and document action is logged and auditable.
